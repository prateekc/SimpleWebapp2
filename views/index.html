About the Application

OnDeman Knowledge Transfer is an application that intends to enable associates to better utilize their time by providing information in a very usable manner and using enhanced search methods to avoid 'Reinventing the Wheel Situation'.

It is basically Smart Visibility and Smart Search in regards to data.
